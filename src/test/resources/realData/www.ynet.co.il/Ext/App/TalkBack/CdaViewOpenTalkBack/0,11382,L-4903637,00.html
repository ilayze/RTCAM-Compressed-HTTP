22. Grouper die from Nylon
sam   (07.01.17)
When i still lived in Israel. i saw from time to time a grouper unable to stay under the water. Grouper are big fish living in groups of 7-8 fish in a cave. One of the fish is a male and the rest are females. They go hunting at night and normally stay in a radius of 20 meters from the hole. If the male dies, one of the femals transforms into male. Nature is a wonderful thing. we must preserve it but the nylon and nylon bags destroy it. One morning after a strom i was walking on the beach and then suddenly I saw a Grouper in agony on the sand. I picked it up and went home where is dissected it. To my surprise, it had a hard stomach - so i was curious to see what it was. When i got it out, i had to open it and when i was finished, it was a 1x1 meter nylon sheet. The poor fish could not digest it , his intake structure made it impossible to vomit it and the anus was too small to eject it. I believe that this is how most predator fish in the world ended and the rest will end as well
